Welcome to AcadSphere

Manage your Students data with AcadSphere!

Key Features:

  • Create: Add new students to the system by providing their details such as name, gender, course, and roll number.
  • Read: View the list of all students along with their information, including student ID, name, gender, course, and roll number.
  • Update: Edit and update the information of existing students, such as their name, gender, etc.
  • Delete: Remove students from the system, permanently deleting their records.
